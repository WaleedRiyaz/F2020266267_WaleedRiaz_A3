Explore My Favorite Places

.

Paris, France:

The "City of Lights" is a perennial favorite for tourists, offering iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre Dame Cathedral. Wander the charming streets of Montmartre, explore the Palace of Versailles, or take a romantic cruise down the Seine River.


London, England:

London is a vibrant metropolis steeped in history and culture. Buckingham Palace, the Houses of Parliament, and Big Ben are some of the recognizable landmarks. Explore world-class museums like the British Museum and the National Gallery, or catch a West End show.


Rome, Italy:

Rome is an open-air museum, filled with ancient ruins like the Colosseum, the Roman Forum, and the Pantheon. Explore Vatican City, home to St. Peter's Square and the Sistine Chapel with Michelangelo's frescoes. Don't miss the Trevi Fountain and toss a coin to ensure your return to this magical city.


New York City, USA:

The "Big Apple" is a city that never sleeps, offering something for everyone. Visit iconic landmarks like the Statue of Liberty, Times Square, and the Empire State Building. Explore world-class museums like the Metropolitan Museum of Art and the Museum of Modern Art, or catch a Broadway show.

Angkor Wat, Cambodia:

Angkor Wat is the world's largest religious monument, a breathtaking temple complex built by the Khmer Empire. Explore the intricate carvings and sprawling grounds of this UNESCO World Heritage Site.




The Great Barrier Reef, Australia:

The world's largest coral reef system is a natural wonder teeming with marine life. Explore the reef through snorkeling, scuba diving, or glass-bottom boat tours. The Great Barrier Reef is a must-see for any nature enthusiast.
