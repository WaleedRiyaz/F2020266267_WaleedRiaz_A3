About Me

Who I Am

My name is Waleed Riaz, and the thrill of crafting digital experiences is what fuels my passion as a Web Developer. After tinkering with code in high school, I knew I wanted to turn that curiosity into a career. I landed my first role at SoftDev Company fresh out of UMT's vibrant computer science program, and the past two years have been a whirlwind of learning and growth. One project that truly shaped my perspective was building an interactive museum exhibit. Collaborating with historians and designers, I witnessed the power of technology to bridge the gap between the past and present. It solidified my belief that web development isn't just about code, it's about creating connections and igniting imaginations. Now, I'm driven by the challenge of crafting user-friendly and engaging websites that not only function flawlessly but also leave a lasting impression.
